Our Story


Catering With Distinction has been serving the appetite of Boston's Corporate, Medical, and Educational institutions for over 30 years. In that time, we have not lost our main objective, to bring you delicious food, presented elegantly with the quality of service you expect. In our catering menus, you will find a wide array of offerings to suit your taste.

Every day, we choose the best ingredients New England has to offer, and work diligently to find the best, local purveyors so that each meal is fresh, colorful and delicious.

After 25 years of service and thousands of successful events, we still take pride in the fact that Catering With Distinction is a small company, dedicated to affordable and personal service. I am pleased to speak to you at any time. Book today.

Andrew Korn, Owner

Our Commitment

At Catering with Distinction, we take a local and honest approach to providing the best ingredients. Close relationships with our vendors allows us to source our produce daily from local suppliers, including Ward's Berry Farm; provide fair-trade coffee from Recreo, which is grown on their family farm and roasted locally; and receive our cakes and pastries daily from Boston's best bakeries. And we give back to places by donating meals to Breakthrough Greater Boston, Smart From the Start, Express Yourself, WBUR, and others.
